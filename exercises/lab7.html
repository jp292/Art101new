Nice Block 1. Crazy Eight rapid brainstorming helps promote ideas, especially if you're stuck and not sure how to brainstorm creatively. It can generate numerous ideas when practiced correctly, and the results can be shocking! It also helps with the visual aspect to see your ideas on paper; it can be a good rough draft for a future final idea. It is a great method for getting the team more comfortable with sharing ideas. It is not verbal at first, so it helps ease those who may not be comfortable speaking out, to then later at the end share and discuss.
